Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

You take I-95 north to Maine then get off at the Belfast exit. Turn left off the exit and go 10 miles. Turn right at the big red barn then go another 5 miles. You will see the Unplugged sign on your left.

What kid of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Any team that is looking for a space to grow closer and improve their communication skills while also enjoying nature.

What size teams can you accommodate?

We work with any size team from 2 people up to 100 people teams.

Is there really no wifi available?

The purpose of this retreat as see in the name is to unplug. We want your team to have every chance to be away from the tech of everyday and grow together. We do have some emergency exceptions but we want everyone to remain unplugged during their stay with us.
